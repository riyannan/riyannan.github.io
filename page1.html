Best Recipe for Chocolate Chip Cookies!

Recipe by Martha Stewart

January 7, 2018

This "Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookie" Recipe Makes 24 Cookies in 1 hr 45 min Martha Stewart Encourages Incorporating Your Own Additions!

  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar

  • 1 1/2 Cups Light-Brown Sugar

  • 1 1/2 Teaspoons Vanilla
