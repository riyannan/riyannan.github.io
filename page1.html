Best Recipe for Chocolate Chip Cookies!

Recipe by Martha Stewart

January 7, 2018

This "Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookie" Recipe Makes 24 Cookies in 1 hr 45 min Martha Stewart Encourages Incorporating Your Own Additions!

Ingredients
  • 3 1/2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 1/4 Teaspooons Baking Soda
  • 1 1/4 Teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1 1/4 Cups Unsalted Butter
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 1 1/2 Cups Light-Brown Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoons Vanilla
  • 8 Ounces Chocolate
Instructions
  1. Mix your flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt together.
  2. Beat your remaining ingredients together with an electric mixer. Add the ingredients in the of butter and sugar first, then eggs, then vanilla, and lastly, chocolate, respectively.
  3. After adding the beaten ingredients with the dry mixture together, place your dough onto parchment paper in 1/4 cup dollops.
  4. Bake 18 minutes for each batch.
  5. Now you cookies are ready!
