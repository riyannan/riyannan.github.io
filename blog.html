The Beatles

The infamous Beatles were "banded" (; together in Liverpool, England through a course of events that can only be described as destiny.

Paul McCartney and George Harrison (lead guitarist) met in school, through riding the same bus. Eventually, Paul came across John Lennon and his band The Quarrymen

McCartney convinced George to show John his guitar skills, and eventually the three were all apart of the band. At first, their band had a different

drummer, but they soon switched Pete Best out for Ringo Starr, and the rest is history
